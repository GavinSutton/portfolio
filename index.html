    Some History

    Hello again. Thanks for scrolling.

    I have a weird story for being such an ordinary person, but I suppose it's because I like my order with a splash of chaos. I won't pretend web development was my plan A, B, or even C (Rockstar, Writer, Marry Rich, in that order). But I love puzzles. I like to fiddle, tinker, and fix. I'm obsessed with making things work. That's why web development tickles my fancy. Unlike my purely creative pursuits, web development has the right and wrong answers I crave after all the years of subjectivity.

    So I took the plunge into that is web dev. I've had my toe in for years, doing HTML and CSS work as a Content Strategist at a Toronto agency, but not diving in until I went to Juno. In those years of contemplation, I wrote songs and toured with a band, lived in England and received an MFA in Creative Writing, wrote a book and explored the world of publishing, and worked briefly as a Communications Specialist for the Government of Nunavut in Iqaluit. All to say, I loved to learn, and I thrive in a fast-paced, ever-changing world - or, aka, web development.
