    Some History

    Hello again. Thanks for scrolling.

    For such an ordinary person, I've lived a weird life, but I suppose it's because I like my order with a splash of chaos. I won't pretend web development was my plan A, B, or even C (Rockstar, Writer, and Marry Rich. Preferably all three). But I love puzzles. I like to tinker, fiddle, and fix. I'm obsessed with making things work. That's why web development tickles my fancy. Unlike my purely creative pursuits, web development has the right and wrong answers I crave after all the years of subjectivity.

    So I took the plunge into the waters of web dev. I had my toe in it for years before I did, doing HTML and CSS work as a Content Strategist at a Toronto agency, but not fully diving in until I began taking courses at Juno College in 2019. In those years of contemplation, I wrote songs and toured with a band, lived in England, received an MFA in Creative Writing, wrote a book, and worked briefly as a Communications Specialist for the Government of Nunavut in Iqaluit. All to say, I love to learn, and I thrive in a fast-paced, ever-changing world. Or, you know, web development.
